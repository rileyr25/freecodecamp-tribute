Who Is Israel Adesanya?

Isreal is a professional figher from Lagos, Nigeria. Adesanya is currently the Middleweight Champion of the UFC.

Fighting Strengths

  1. Striking
  2. Speed
  3. Kicks

Career Highlights

  • Won King of the Ring tournament in Auckland, New Zeland.
  • Became Pro in 2012, fighting in Hong Kong and Australia for the next 5 years.
  • Isreal signed with the UFC in 2017, debuting against Rob Wilkinson.
    He won his debut in the second round by TKO.
  • Made his way through the ranks of the UFC becoming the
    Middleweight Champion in 2019, winning against
    Kevin Gastelum by unanimous decision.
  • Adesanya is now the defending Champion of the UFC Middleweight division.
